Gaming

Valorant

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter by Riot Games where precise aim and team strategy determine the outcome. I used to play competitively in the collegiate scene and at my peak I was ranked in the top 0.1% of players in North America.

Auburn University Varsity Team

I was a member of Auburn University's Valorant varsity esports team. Competing at the collegiate level meant regular scrimmages, organized team practice, and tournaments against other universities across the country. It was a great experience combining my love of the game with representing Auburn.

What Makes Valorant Competitive

Unlike casual games, high-level Valorant requires:
  • Precise mechanical aim under pressure
  • Deep knowledge of agent abilities and team compositions
  • Map control, economy management, and real-time communication
